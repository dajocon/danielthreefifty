Hannah Lee \
How much time is left to be \
Here with me?

Pretty, pretty all too soon \
We all see our last full moon \
As you ascend up to Thee

Death comes for us all \
But I promise to stand tall

Father, into your hands I commend my spirit \
The grace you gave me to inherit \
Always giving new life \
The promise of eternal life \
For my God given wife 

Hannah is taken from the TV show Dark. She is a character who does not love the man who she is having an affair with, but has an intense infatuation with him. She represents someone who cannot love because she doesn't love God. Lee is taken from the book East of Eden as a character that talks about how we can overcome pain, trials, and temptations when we set our mind to it. Taken as a composite, Hannah Lee, the name means we can love wholey when we first love God.
